About Us

Welcome to Amity University Rajasthan's (A.U.R.) annual spectacle, Odyssey - a dynamic blend of culture and technology spanning three exhilarating days. With over 50+ events, Odyssey caters to every passion, ensuring students find their niche in a vibrant array of cultural and tech competitions. From electrifying performances to cutting-edge tech challenges, this fest celebrates diversity and inclusivity. Participation is not just encouraged but celebrated, creating an atmosphere of healthy competition and collaboration. Join us for a journey where talents shine, friendships blossom, and aspirations take center stage. Let's make Odyssey at A.U.R. a three-day ode to creativity, innovation, and camaraderie!
